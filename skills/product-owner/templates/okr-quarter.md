## How to score

Score each Key Result at end of quarter on a **0.0 – 1.0** scale:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 0.7 – 1.0 | On target or exceeded — healthy stretch |
| 0.4 – 0.69 | Progress made but fell short |
| 0.0 – 0.39 | Missed — review whether target was realistic or focus drifted |

A team consistently scoring 1.0 set targets too conservatively.
A team consistently scoring < 0.4 needs to review capacity or target calibration.
